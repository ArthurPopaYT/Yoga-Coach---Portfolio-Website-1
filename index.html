About Me

Hello, I'm Emma Green, a certified yoga instructor with over 5 years of experience guiding people through their yoga journey. My approach combines traditional techniques with modern wellness science to create a practice that nurtures both body and mind.

After discovering yoga during a challenging time in my life, I experienced firsthand the transformative power of this ancient practice. This inspired me to obtain certifications in Hatha, Vinyasa, and Restorative yoga to share these benefits with others.

My teaching philosophy centers on making yoga accessible to everyone, regardless of age, fitness level, or experience. I believe yoga is not just about physical postures, but a holistic practice that promotes mental clarity, emotional balance, and spiritual growth.
